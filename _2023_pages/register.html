Conference Fee

The conference fee is 25 Euros and includes:

  • Accommodation from June 28th to July 2nd
  • Breakfast at the accommodation
  • Lunch in the Mensa on campus (vegetarian & vegan options) on Thursday and Friday, catering on Saturday

Participation in the Sunday Brunch is not included.
