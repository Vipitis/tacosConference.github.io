Topics of Interest

We welcome abstracts for presentations (20 minutes) and workshops (1 hour), on broadly the following main topics:

  • Computational Linguistics
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Digital Humanities with a linguistics component
  • Computer Science

All work that you find interesting or are proud of can be submitted! Your bachelors or masters thesis, a term-paper, work done as a student assistent or simply a hobby project.

  

Submission instructions

Abstracts may be submitted in English or German and should contain a body of around 300 words excluding title and references and should be submitted as PDF. Abstracts can be submitted via EasyChair once the call for papers opens on March 20th.
