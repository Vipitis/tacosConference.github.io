We currently offer 6 diversity scholarships á 75€ each, sponsored by Digital Humanities and IGDD. The scholarships will be sent out after the conference, directly by our sponsors.

Diversity Scholarship by Digital Humanities

Two general diversity scholarships which support anyone who needs aid to attend the conference, be it partcipants with special needs, participants who require financial aid, participants from far away, and others.

The following criteria will, among others, impact the selection process:

  • Distance between your home university to the TaCoS venue
  • First-time participation
  • No financial support of your university
  • Your abstract’s quality

    •   

    Diversity Scholarship by IGDD

    Four scholarships for students whose talks are delving into the topics of language variety, ideally in German language variety.

    How to apply

    Applications incl. abstract and short motivation statement can be sent in until April 17, 2022 via https://anmeldung.stuts.de/StuTS_Stipendium. The scholarships will be awarded by April 24, 2022. We will notify you prior to the conference, whether you were selected to receive one of the scholarships. We will then relay you to our sponsors and attest that you have visited the conference. You can then thank our sponsors and exchange information to receive the scholarship.

    Contact

    If you have any further questions regarding the call for papers you can send us a mail at tacos2023@linguistik.computer.
