Important Dates

The call for participation has been extended! You can submit your presentation here!
The new deadline is April 24th.
     

Topics of Interest

We welcome abstracts for presentations (20 minutes) and workshops (1 hour), on broadly the following main topics:

  • Computational Linguistics
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Digital Humanities with a linguistics component
  • Computer Science

All work that you find interesting or are proud of can be submitted! Your bachelors or masters thesis, a term-paper, work done as a student assistent or simply a hobby project.

  

Submission instructions

Abstracts may be submitted in English or German and should contain a body of around 300 words excluding title. Abstracts can be submitted via the link below.

Below are some intructions that should be helpful:

  1. Make sure you have registered for the conference.
  2. Follow the link to get to the Call for participation: 33. TaCoS CfP.
    Note: If you don't have an account yet, create an account and follow the link again.
  3. Click on Submit a new event.
  4. When filling out the form, make sure to specify the event type and the language, as well as write an abstract or around 300 words in the description section.
  5. You may also submit additional information by attaching links/files and writing in the submission notes.

    6. We are excited to read your abstracts and we hope to see you soon at Saarland University!

  

Contact

If you have any further questions regarding the call for participation you can send us a mail at tacos2024@linguistik.computer.
